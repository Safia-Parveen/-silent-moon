Silent Moon

A symbol of peace, silence and quite hope.

Introduction

The Moon is Earth’s only natural satellite.It looks calm and peaceful in the night sky, like a quiet hope shining in darkness.

🌊 Moon Basic Information

Feature Information
Diameter(size) 3,474 km

Orbital Period 27.3 days
Surface Craters, Mountains, Valleys
Colour White/Pale Yellow

🌌 Effect of Moon at Night

The moon’s gravity pulls water on Earth, making high and low tides.At night, the moon can make the sea calm or strong depending on its position.

🌟 Facts

  • The Moon has no atmosphere, so there is no sound.
  • Its gravity is 1/6th of Earth’s gravity.
  • The same side of the Moon always faces the Earth.
